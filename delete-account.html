Account & Data Deletion

Developer: Phani Peddibhotla  ·  App: Impulse  ·  Last Updated: April 23, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Impulse account and the data associated with it. We are committed to respecting your right to remove your personal information from our services.

1. How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your Impulse account and associated data, please send an email with the following details:

Email to: phani.peddibhotla@gmail.com

Subject line: Impulse Account Deletion Request

In the email body, include:

  • The Google account email you used to sign in to Impulse
  • A clear statement requesting account deletion (e.g., "I request that my Impulse account and all associated data be permanently deleted")

Once we receive your request, we will confirm receipt within 72 hours and complete the deletion within 30 days. You will receive a final confirmation by email once deletion is complete.

2. What Gets Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account profile (email, name, and profile photo linked from Google Sign-In)
  • Any deals you posted, including photos you uploaded
  • Your tracking list (deals you saved for price-drop alerts)
  • Your confirmations and flags on other deals
  • Your Firebase Cloud Messaging token (used for push notifications)
  • Your user preferences (search radius, notification settings, theme)
  • Firebase Authentication record

3. What May Be Retained

In some cases, certain information is retained for legal, operational, or safety reasons:

  • Aggregated analytics data: Firebase Analytics may have already aggregated anonymized usage patterns (screens viewed, features used) that cannot be linked back to your account after deletion. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Content integrity records: If you previously flagged a deal as inappropriate, the flag itself may be kept (without your name or account details) to preserve content moderation history.
  • Legal compliance: We may retain limited information if required by applicable law (e.g., records of a reported abuse case, court order).

We do not sell user data to third parties. No retained data is used for advertising or resold after deletion.

4. Timeline

  1. Within 72 hours of receiving your email — we will confirm receipt of your deletion request.
  2. Within 30 days of receiving your email — we will complete the deletion and notify you by email.
  3. Beyond 30 days — only data covered in "What May Be Retained" (Section 3) will remain.

5. Alternatives to Deletion

If you want to stop using Impulse but are not ready to delete your account, you have these options:

  • Sign out: Sign out of your Google account in the Profile tab. Your data stays, but you're no longer signed in.
  • Uninstall the app: Removes the app from your device but keeps your server-side data.
  • Disable notifications: Turn off push notifications in your device settings or in the app's Profile tab.
  • Revoke location access: Turn off location permission in your device settings — your location is no longer collected.
