Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: April 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Impulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of the Impulse mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the App:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account.
  • Location Data: We collect your device's GPS location to show deals near you. Location is used in real time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Device Token: We store a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token on our servers to send you push notifications about price drops and new deals.
  • Deal Activity: When you track a deal or post a deal, that information (including any photos you upload) is stored in our database.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage information may be collected by Firebase Analytics to help us improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalize the Impulse experience
  • Show you deals relevant to your location and preferences
  • Send you push notifications about price drops and new deals (only if you opt in)
  • Allow you to post, track, and share deals with the community
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase to store and process data. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Push Notifications

If you grant permission, Impulse may send you push notifications about price drops on deals you are tracking, new deals in your area, and app updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device's Settings app.

6. Children's Privacy

Impulse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time
  • Withdraw consent for location access through your device settings

To request account deletion or data access, please contact us at the email below.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, storage, and notifications
  • Google Sign-In — Account authentication
